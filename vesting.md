# CLP Vesting Architecture

CLP follows a structured long-term vesting model.

## Founder Allocation (30%)

- 4,000,000 CLP operational reserve  
  - 1 month cliff  
  - 12 month linear unlock (~333,333 CLP/month)

- 25,000,000 CLP long-term reserve  
  - 24 month lock  
  - 500,000 CLP/month unlock after lock

---

## Treasury (35%)

- 12 month lock
- 700,000 CLP/month linear unlock after lock

---

## Marketing & Ecosystem (25%)

- 12 month lock
- 500,000 CLP/month linear unlock after lock

---

All vesting is structured for long-term ecosystem growth and stability.
